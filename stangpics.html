Here is my interior. No, it is not orange, although it sort of looks like it. It is red and black. As you can see, my CD player has been replaced with a Sony XPlod M620 ABP player. The racing pedals can be seen, along with the 35th Anniversary gauge bezel in the dash.

Here is a picture of my rear end. As you can see, the entire tailpipe is chrome, not just the tips. This is the right side of the car. I have duals so it looks the same on the other side. It had been raining so everything is kind of dirty!
Now the seat is down. As you can see, my subwoofer amplifier is bolted onto the back of it. The amplifier is a Sony XPlod XM-1502SX and puts out 760 heart-thumping watts into one 12" Sony XPlod L-1236 subwoofer.
Here is a pic of my car on the outside. I have the White 17" Cobra R rims with Kumho Ecsat Supra Ultra High Performance tires. They are 245/45/ZR17's. I have converted the tail-lights in my car to the vertical lenses with all red. The lenses are from a 96-98 year Stang. The bumper is filled in with "MUSTANG" and I have pinstripes on the side of the car. Dual MAC exhaust can be seen (cat-backs) with chrome 2.5" tailpipes.
This is a close-up of my front driver-side panel. The clear turn signals are in and you can see the pinstripes and the white windsheild wipers now. I also put Xenon headlights in (like the Lexus' and Benz's) and fog lights. The tire and rim is also visible.
